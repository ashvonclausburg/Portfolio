08 — Problem Cards & Hypothesis

Problem 01

Unclear trigger

Sellers don't understand why their account is frozen or what action is needed from them.

Problem 02

Wrong timing

The verification request arrives after the seller has already tried to take a payment.

Hypothesis

If we prompt sellers to verify their identity before they hit a freeze, they will complete the flow at a higher rate and experience less churn.

09 — Finding & Recommendation Cards

Key Finding

73% of sellers who saw the proactive prompt completed identity verification within 48 hours — compared to 31% in the reactive condition.

Rec 01

Surface the prompt earlier

Show the verification request at onboarding, not at the point of payment failure.

Rec 02

Reduce copy friction

Replace legal-heavy language with plain, action-oriented instructions.
